## Analytical Insights

### Store Performance
- **Top Cities:** Peshawar, Sialkot, Islamabad, Rawalpindi  
- **Bottom Cities:** Karachi, Faisalabad, Quetta  
- **Revenue Range:** 5.2M – 6.4M per top store

### Promotion Effectiveness
- **Highest Revenue:** 500 Cashback (**+122.6M**)
- **Highest Units:** BOGOF (**+157K** units)
- **Lowest Performers:** 25% OFF, 50% OFF

### Product Category Performance
- **Most Responsive:** Grocery & Staples (+14.9M revenue)
- **Moderate:** Personal Care (+654K revenue)  
- **Negative Impact:** Home Care (-348K revenue)

---

## Business Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
1. **Prioritize Cashback and BOGOF** promotions over percentage discounts
2. **Focus on Grocery & Staples** and **Personal Care** categories
3. **Phase out 25% OFF and 50% OFF** underperforming campaigns

### Strategic Initiatives
4. **Replicate successful strategies** from top-performing cities
5. **Implement city-specific** promotional strategies
6. **Optimize resource allocation** based on regional responsiveness
